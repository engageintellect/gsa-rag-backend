General Description:

Blue Glacier Management Group Inc. (Blue Glacier) is a highly experienced cybersecurity, systems engineering, and information technology firm that is focused on providing subject matter expertise to better enable and support all of our client missions. Blue Glacier assists United States Federal Agencies, the Department of Defense, and the Intelligence Community in developing and implementing innovative solutions to improve their infrastructure security and management of those programs that support mission requirements. We fully incorporate people, processes, and technologies to fully align with each client’s strategic plans in the cybersecurity and technology arenas. Blue Glacier’s subject matter expertise include supporting both Chief Information Officers (CIOs) and Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs) in developing and implementing policies, program management of CIO/CISO programs, and directly supporting operational programs such as 24x7x365 Security Operations Centers (SOCs) and Network Operations Centers (NOCs), Enterprise Architecture; information technology architecture design, implementation, and integration; and business process reengineering.

BASIC GUIDELINES FOR USING "CONTRACTOR TEAM ARRANGEMENTS"‌



Federal Supply Schedule Contractors may use "Contractor Team Arrangements" (see FAR 9.6) to provide solutions when responding to an ordering activity requirement.


These Team Arrangements can be included under a Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA). BPAs are permitted under all Federal Supply Schedule contracts.


Orders under a Team Arrangement are subject to terms and conditions or the Federal Supply Schedule Contract.


Participation in a Team Arrangement is limited to Federal Supply Schedule Contractors. Customers should refer to FAR 9.6 for specific details on Team Arrangements.

Here is a general outline on how it works:

  • The customer identifies their requirements.

  • Federal Supply Schedule Contractors may individually meet the customer’s needs, or -

  • Federal Supply Schedule Contractors may individually submit a Schedules "Team Solution" to meet the customer's requirement.

  • Customers make a best value selection.
